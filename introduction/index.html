Bakery


The Bakery is a production building unlocked at experience level 2.
It is the first production building players unlock in the game.
It is used to make bread, cookies, muffins and pizza


Barn


The barn is the main storage building for products, animal goods and supplies.
It is already standing at the start of the game, but must be repaired during the tutorial to become usable.
